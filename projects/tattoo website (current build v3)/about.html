About The Artist

Meet Conrad

Conrad focuses on custom work with strong line confidence, flow-aware placement, and long-term readability. He works one-on-one with each client to turn rough ideas into wearable designs.

Based in Auckland and available to travel for larger projects, his sessions prioritize thoughtful planning, clean execution, and an easy studio experience from first message to healed result.
